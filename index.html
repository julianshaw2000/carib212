About

Situated within five acres of tropical trees, well-maintained grounds and lots of lush tropical flora on the Ocho Rios Bay, this one+ bedroom apartment has air-conditioning in the main bedroom with FREE INTERNET ACCESS. The accommodation is tastefully appointed for a comfortable and enjoyable stay and includes access to the private beach. The large balcony provides panoramic views of the Ocho Rios Bay. The property has 24 hour security and a housekeeper who will meet and greet you upon your arrival.

Around Us

Blue Hole: To the south of Ocho Rios, hidden in the looming mountains, lie pools of crystalline turquoise waters, which beckon to you to take a leap and immerse yourself in promises of a cool and refreshing swim. Blue hole, often called Irie Blue Hole,  is arguably the most visited yet undeveloped tourist attraction in Ocho Rios, St. Ann. It easily holds its own against the region’s most noteworthy attractions in the eyes of tourists and locals alike.

Mystic Mountain: This attraction offers 3 main attractions Sky Explore, Bobsled Jamaica and Zip-line.

Sky Explorer is a state-of-the-art chairlift that sends guests soaring 700ft above the treetops to the top of Mystic Mountain.

Bobsled Jamaica, the park’s signature attraction, features a 1000m long gravity-drive through the rainforest.

Zip-line, a secure attraction, whisks travelers through the verdant rainforest canopy.

Chukka Park Rides: Soar through the jungle rain-forest on our extreme zip-lines. Step aboard our catamaran cruise for the ultimate party experience and climb the magnificent Dunn’s River Falls. Relax beach-side or try one of our new pools. Snorkel a stunning reef alongside stingrays! Enjoy the original Horseback Ride ‘n’ Swim in the warm Caribbean Sea. Go tubing down a river or through a mysterious limestone cave. Discover our true culture as you travel through countryside and history-rich communities. Tour the home of Bob Marley, the King of Reggae. Try authentic food and sample the legendary local rum.

White River Rafting: Rafting down the White River through the rain-forest. Enjoy nature at its best as you meander through this hidden paradise just seven miles outside of bustling Ocho Rios, Jamaica. The calm surroundings greet you at White River Valley and the consistency of the river flow makes it stand out above other locations on the island. Enjoy the call of the exotic birds which have retreated to the sanctuary of the forest. This is a tour for everyone.

Calabash Ital Restaraunt: The Best Veggie Restaurant in Ochi! Food is always fresh, rich, and filling. Affordable meal for any visitor and hearty experience for any Jamaican. For the last year I've been showing both foreigners and Jamaicans this gem tucked near the center of town and have consistently been delighted. Even a person with the most carnivorous palate will find a flavorful meal inside.

Miss Ts: The oxtail stew here is famous, but that curry goat looks good too. And there are so many ways to have your fish or meat prepared: steamed, escoveitch, brown stew, grilled, rundown, curried…. There are no walls at Miss T’s—just a roof held up by tree-like posts decorated with red fairy lights.
